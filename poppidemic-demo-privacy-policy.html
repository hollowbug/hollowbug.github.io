Poppidemic Demo — Privacy Policy

Updated: 2025-09-24

Summary

This application (Poppidemic Demo) does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data or usage data about its users. The app works without registration and does not use external analytics or advertising services.

What we do not collect

  • Email addresses, names, or other identifying information
  • User-specific browsing or usage logs
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Any other personal data

Third-party services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Device permissions and local data

This app does not request or use any device permissions (such as location, contacts, camera, or microphone). Your progress in the game is only saved locally to your device. We do not collect or transmit any data.

Logs and temporary data

Some platforms or providers may generate technical logs (e.g., error reports or service events). In this project we do not collect personal log entries nor associate technical logs with individual users.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not perform age verification.

Your rights

Because we do not process personal data, there is no personal data to delete or export from this app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Updates will be reflected on this page and the date will be updated to show the latest change.
